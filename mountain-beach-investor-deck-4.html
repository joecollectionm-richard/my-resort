Market Intelligence · 2025–2026

Why Pattaya. Why Now.

Pattaya is undergoing a structural transformation — from budget beach resort to a MICE-driven, upper-midscale destination supercharged by Eastern Economic Corridor (EEC) infrastructure. Branded hotels are winning decisively.

📊 ฿3,498 Pattaya Market ADR · 2024

Pattaya's 2024 average daily rate across all classes. Branded upper-midscale properties (Holiday Inn-tier) achieved significantly higher ADRs of ฿4,000+, demonstrating the premium IHG branding commands in this market.

🛏️ 71% Average Occupancy · Pattaya 2024

The market achieved 71% occupancy with 31,581 keys across 141 properties. Branded hotels with loyalty programmes (IHG One Rewards) consistently outperform independents — projecting 80% post-rebranding.

✈️ EEC Eastern Economic Corridor Catalyst

U-Tapao Airport expansion and EEC industrial investment are driving sustained corporate MICE demand. Pattaya is actively pivoting from leisure to a dual leisure-MICE destination, benefiting convention-capable properties.

🏷️ +10.7% APAC Visitor Growth · H1 2025

UN Tourism recorded 10.7% international visitation growth across Asia-Pacific in H1 2025. Russia led Pattaya arrivals in 2024, with Indian and Chinese markets growing rapidly — all key IHG loyalty segments.

🔄 Rebrand Industry Mega-Trend

Across APAC, operators are rebranding existing hotels rather than building new ones due to high construction costs. Conversion to Holiday Inn delivers rate premiums without greenfield risk. IHG's global network of 100M+ members drives immediate RevPAR uplift.

💰 ฿5,873 Luxury ADR Benchmark · Thailand 2024

Thailand's luxury hotel segment achieved ฿5,873 ADR in 2024 — illustrating the rate ceiling available to well-positioned, branded assets on Pratumnak Hill as the neighbourhood continues to attract premium investment.

Six Pillars of the
Investment Thesis

01
Deep Value Acquisition

Acquiring at ฿2.83M/key versus replacement cost of ฿5.2–5.6M/key — a structural margin of safety that is virtually impossible to replicate in today's construction cost environment. Building this hotel today costs nearly twice the asking price.

02
Brand Catalyst (IHG / Holiday Inn)

IHG's 100M+ loyalty member base, global GDS connectivity, and proven Thailand conversion track record will immediately lift occupancy, ADR, and MICE revenue. Brand affiliation eliminates OTA dependency and drives direct bookings.

03
Premium Micro-Location

Pratumnak Hill is Pattaya's most prestigious address — home to Royal Cliff (5★), InterContinental (5★), and a growing cluster of luxury mixed-use developments. The neighbourhood uplifts brand perception and supports premium rate positioning.

04
Irreplaceable Freehold Asset

Freehold land title in one of Thailand's premier resort destinations — a vanishingly rare characteristic. Combined with 8,000 sqm of freehold land, this provides the foundation for long-term compounding value and future development optionality.

05
EEC Infrastructure Tailwind

Eastern Economic Corridor investments, U-Tapao Airport expansion, and Pattaya's emerging MICE positioning structurally underpin corporate demand growth for the next decade — exactly the demand segment Holiday Inn captures most effectively.

06
Multiple Exit Pathways

Investors benefit from three distinct exit routes: (1) stabilised branded hotel sale at 7–8% cap rate; (2) branded serviced residence conversion/strata sale; or (3) long-term income hold. Each pathway delivers compelling returns on the ฿900M basis.
